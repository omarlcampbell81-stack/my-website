About Birth Loop

Where birth preparation becomes a celebration

Birth Loop is a gamified, immersive learning experience that transforms the traditional approach to birth preparation. Funded by the Thinc Fund and currently piloted across South London, we bridge health equity gaps through play.

Using a board-game-based methodology, participants build health literacy and advocacy skills in a warm, celebratory setting — navigating their own learning pathway through the maternity journey.

Every session is supported by a Midwife, Professional Counsellor, and Peer Supporters, ensuring both clinical expertise and lived-experience wisdom are present for every family.
