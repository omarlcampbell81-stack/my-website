About Birth Loop

Where birth preparation becomes a celebration

Imagine an escape room designed around your birth journey. Birth Loop is a large-scale, immersive board game experience that transforms how families prepare for one of the biggest moments of their lives. Think tactile challenges, live scenarios, problem-solving under pressure and real midwife expertise — all in one celebratory space.

You navigate a colour-coded game board, choose your own path through the maternity journey, and unlock knowledge through play. Food and drink are provided throughout, culturally sensitive and delicious. You leave with a Confidence Toolkit, vouchers, and everything you need to feel ready.
