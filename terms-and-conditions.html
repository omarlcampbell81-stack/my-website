2. Our Sessions

Birth Loop sessions are free to attend and open to expectant parents, partners, and supporters in South London. Sessions are currently held in Brixton and Peckham.

Sessions are educational and experiential in nature. They are not a substitute for medical advice, clinical care, or professional midwifery services.

Session formats include Women-Only, Couples, and Men-Only. Please ensure you select the appropriate format when booking.
